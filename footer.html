About Modtech

Modtech is a bot developed by 3 people, and aims to be one of the best moderation bots out there. It was founded in April 2020 by Jamieee, who no longer works on the bot, and now is maintained by Morgan with some assistance from 0J3 and Marcus N. Modtech is developed exclusively in our free time, so any donation would be hugely appreciated.
